History of Neptune

In the early days of The Empire of Neptune, there was some chaos.

Pretty much immediately Gremigunk came by to establish formal relations. They had some disagreements, mainly due to both claiming Ceres but that got resolved pretty quickly. Gremigunk did consider waging war at first but they ultimately did not want to do so, and thus Emily established their relations instead.

However it wouldn't take long for the stability of these relations to be tested, as shortly afterwards Flex decided to capture Plex's robot named Orian. Flex wanted to deconstruct it, but was ultimately stopped after a whole group of people had to go after her. It almost led to a war, but luckily it never got so far.

A while later, new drama struck. Vesper took over Thalassa (a moon of Neptune) after The Empire of Neptune did not agree to sell land on it. Gremigunk's forces where immediately called in to counter-attack. Whilst that was going on, a deal was made justify-content-between The Empire of Neptune and The Saturnian Empire and they gave up asteroids Palles & Vesta in exchange for Vesper leaving Thalassa.

Since then, there hasn't been much major history in The Empire of Neptune. A war almost happened over Titan, which you can read about here. They are also involved in the Dyson Swarm project.
