The Empire of Neptune

The Empire of Neptune is a large empire within The Solar System.

Members:

The Empire of Neptune has 4 members and 4 civilians.
Jade, who is the president and founder of the empire.
Lena, who is the branch manager on Ceres and the lead historian.
Ama, who is the Pluto affairs manager.
Hackleberry, who is the minister of public affairs.

Civilians:
Kathii, who owns the witch cafe.
Mia, who is a chaos civilian.
Jolyne, who has received a tour of Neptune.
Log, who is a regular civilian.

Territory:

They span across the neptunian system and they own Pluto, Titan and Io.
Though after the Saturnian Empire expressed desire to expand into Jupiter, suddenly The Empire of Neptune has made claims about owning the entire Jupiter System.
They secretly own Kepler 22b as well, which is their only territory outside The Solar System.
Lastly, they share a portion of Ceres with Gremigunk.

Military:

The Empire of Neptune doesnt have a traditional military. They mostly have nukes, which is their main weapon.
Since recent events though, Lena has been building a security force on Ceres, in cooperation with Luna. This force may eventually become the official army of The Empire of Neptune.

Alliances:

The Empire of Neptune is in a good relationship with Gremigunk, having a full aliance with them.
They also work together with the Saturnian Empire and Mars, though those relations are not as established and a bit more unstable.
