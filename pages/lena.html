Lena

History:

Lena is a member of The Empire of Neptune.
She is the lead historian and Neptune Treaty Manager on Ceres.

She has a twin sister in Gremigunk, named Luna. Both of them share a portion of Ceres with each other. They have agreed to work together on military, economic and many other things as a team. Recently, they managed to convince both of their nations to make Ceres a fully shared territory between them which is governed by the both of them.

Lastly she leads the Ceres Armed Forces, which is the only known military unit within The Empire of Neptune.
