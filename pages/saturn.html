Saturnian Empire

The Saturnian Empire is a empire build around the planet Saturn.

Members:

Saturn has only 1 known member:
Vesper, she is the empress of saturn.

Territory:

The Saturnian Empire spans trough The Solar System.
They own everything around Saturn, except Titan. They also own half a dwarf galaxy and dwarf planet Orcus.

Military:

Saturn's military has 578 million soldiers, and a vast arry of weapons. Their exact capabilities are unknown.

Relations:

The Saturnian Empire is currently peacefull with The Empire of Neptune and Gremigunk. Though they have somewhat shaky relations at times.

Other Info:

The exact date of when the Empire was established is unknown, though it was first mentioned on 13-05-2025.
