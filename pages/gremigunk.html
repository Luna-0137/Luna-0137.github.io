Gremigunk

Gremigunk is a intergalactic empire that spans everywhere.

Members:

Gremigunk has 6 members, 1 ex-member and several acquaintances.
Jenny, who is the president and founder of the empire.
Emily, who is the foreign relations expert.
Closet Dwelling, who leads the military.
Luna, who is the lead historian, vice military commander and owner of Ceres.
8_8, who is a witch.
Ama, who owns Pluto.

Plex, who was a weapon engineer and military advisor.

Acquaintances:
Nikki, who once wanted war.
Cthulhu, who is Jenny's Wife.
Luigi, who assisinated the healthcare ceo and is the reaper.

Territory:

Gremigunk spans across several galaxies, and has territory almost anywhere.
Troughout The Solar System they own Ceres, the Uranus system and they own most of the dwarf planets.

Military:

Gremigunk's military is vast and contains at least 13 billion femboys, 10 battalions, 69 legions, 2 billion femboy super soldiers and 237 military bases.
The army can also shoot superheated thermal paste at over 725km/h. It used to have the Ebnewp Weapons Program as well until Plex left.
Lastly, they have a lot of nukes as well.

Relations:

Gremigunk is in a good relationship with The Empire of Neptune, having a full aliance with them.
They also work together with the Saturnian Empire and Mars, though those relations are not as established and a bit more unstable.

Other Info:

Gremigunk was established on 08-03-2025. They have a estrogen bank as well. The national currency is frogs.
