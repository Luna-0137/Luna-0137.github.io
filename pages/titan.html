Titan

Titan is a moon orbiting Saturn.

Ownership:

Titan is owned by The Empire of Neptune. Despite claims from The Saturnian Empire that Titan belongs to them, it has stayed in the hands of The Empire of Neptune.
Currently, there is a research base on Titan owned by Vesper.

History:

When The Empire of Neptune was established, they took Titan as part of their territory. Then when The Saturnian Empire came along, they claimed it instead since it was within their natural region. This had caused some minor disagreements but those usually ended fairly quickly.

Though at some Vesper surrounded Titan with military units after she was unable to buy Titan from The Empire of Neptune. Gremigunk took that action as a potential beginning of a invasion and threatened to cancel their alliance with The Saturnian Empire. They managed to calm things down and agree on a research station on Titan.

However, Plex thought that Vesper was untrustworthy and decided to go rogue in order to strike first. The attacks where stopped, but at the damage was done. Gremigunk condemned her actions, and not long after Plex decided to leave Gremigunk due to personal differences. Things calmed down afterwards and today there hasn't been any more fighting over Titan.
