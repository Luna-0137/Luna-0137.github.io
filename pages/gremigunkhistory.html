History of Gremigunk

One of the first major events of Gremigunk was the invasion of the . After Gremigunk found out they where regaining power, an attack on their western border was launched. During this time, rebellions where also being incited. When the troops landed on the western border, they found that the towns where in lockdown and that their tools where primitive. Gremigunk's infantry quikly neutralised the first reponders to the attack.
The decision was made to fire-bomb the southern hemisphere, but the firebombs where incinerated when they went trough a plasma barrier at an military base. Thus raw super-heated plasmothermal paste was shot at the military base at over 725km/h, which destroyed the base and even damaged the planet as a whole. It wiped Gremigunk's infantry out as well, and the planet was damaged to the point estrogen mining operations had to be paused for a week.
